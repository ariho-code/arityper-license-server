How AriTyper Works

Four simple steps from document to fully-typed text — in under two minutes.

01

Generate or Prepare Content

Write your content, use AI to help draft it, or work from any existing document. Format it exactly how you want.

02

Export to PDF or Word

Save your content as a .pdf or .docx file. AriTyper reads both formats while preserving all your formatting.

03

Select Target & Load File

Click into the field or application where you need the text, then import your document into AriTyper.

04

AriTyper Types for You

Hit Start and watch AriTyper type your entire document at lightning speed — up to 2 minutes for full docs.
